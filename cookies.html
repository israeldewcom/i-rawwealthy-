Cookie Policy

Last Updated: December 1, 2023

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences, analyzing site usage, and personalizing content.

2. How We Use Cookies

Raw Wealthy uses cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in to your account
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze how our platform is used
  • Improve security and prevent fraud
  • Provide personalized investment recommendations
  • Measure advertising effectiveness

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for platform functionality, security, and account access Session / 30 days
Functional Cookies Remember preferences, language settings, and display choices 90 days
Analytics Cookies Analyze user behavior to improve platform experience 365 days
Marketing Cookies Track marketing campaign effectiveness (optional) 180 days

4. Essential Cookies Details

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function:

  • session_id: Maintains your login session
  • auth_token: Secures authentication data
  • csrf_token: Preforms cross-site request forgery protection
  • security_check: Enhances platform security

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics
  • Stripe/Paystack: For payment processing
  • Cloudflare: For security and performance
  • Facebook Pixel: For advertising insights (optional)

These third parties have their own privacy policies.

6. Your Cookie Choices

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to manage cookies
  • Cookie Consent Banner: Options when you first visit our site
  • Opt-Out Tools: Industry tools for advertising cookies

Note: Disabling essential cookies may prevent platform functionality.

7. How to Manage Cookies

Browser Controls:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

You can also use private/incognito mode to limit cookie storage.

8. Do Not Track Signals

We currently do not respond to Do Not Track (DNT) signals as there's no industry standard. However, we respect your privacy choices through our cookie consent tool.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through platform notifications or email.
