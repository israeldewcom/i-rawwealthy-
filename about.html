About Raw Wealthy

Empowering Financial Growth Through Raw Materials Investment

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Raw Wealthy began with a simple vision: to democratize access to raw materials investment opportunities. We recognized that while commodities trading has historically been reserved for institutional investors, individual investors also deserved access to these lucrative markets.

Our founders, with combined experience of over 25 years in finance, commodities trading, and technology, set out to create a platform that makes raw materials investment simple, secure, and accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

To empower individuals to build wealth through secure, transparent, and profitable raw materials investments while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and customer service.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading platform for raw materials investment, connecting investors with carefully curated opportunities while contributing to economic development through responsible investment practices.
